What we are all about?
A very warm welcome to this awesome website. This is a business demo (demonstration) where you will learn all you need to know to survive in this business world. The world is like a global market, where transactions are done on a daily basis even as we speak. But remember that the world is going digital, that means more opportunities, and more opportunities brings more competition.

Then you may ask, why are we here? We are here because of you. We have got all it takes for you to survive in such a competitive environment. We would teach you how to market your goods, how to advertise your products to reach your target audience, and how to make multiple sales.

PS: This is not a get rich quick scheme where you may think that after you have being fully trained, you will end up with millions in a week. Every successfully business started from somewhere. It may take time and dedication but it will eventually pay off. It's time for you to change your thinking, it's time that you no longer work hard but work smart.

What we offer?
To enable you have access to an effective learning environment, we added some extra features to make it more real. You have a single bank account and we woud be trading using the USD ($). Here are a list of a few noble addition:

  • Analysis page: Every successful business takes in to account their sales, and also note their best performing product and that which is incuring more loss on them. In there, you can monitor your progress and track your profit and losses.

    Check out Sales analysis.

  • Products section: This is where your products which include your goods and services can be found. Always note that you might not be the only one selling a particular kind of product, so you must try to stand out amongst them in order to make high sales.

    Check out Your products.

  • Trade section: This is basically where all your finances are stored. You will be given an initial capital of $10,000 as a first timer, and it is left for you to practice all you have learnt in the tutorial, purchase the right products and make sales at the right time to make profits instead of incuring losses.

    Check out Start trading.

  • Business news: Stay up to date with the latest business news, and find out what's happening in the international market. But it's also important to note, that this is a business demo (demonstration), so the news that will be broadcasted are not from the real world, but just demonstrations to see how well you stay up-to-date, and how well you respond to latest changes in the market system.

    Check out Latest news (coming soon).
